Interactive Movie Games

Overview:
Interactive movies blur the line between cinema and gaming, emphasizing storytelling over traditional mechanics. These games present players with movie-like visuals and narratives where choices shape the outcome. Instead of skill-based combat, the tension often comes from dialogue choices, quick-time events, or branching paths. The goal is immersion—players feel like they are living inside a film where their decisions matter. They’re perfect for players who enjoy narrative-driven experiences and emotional storytelling.


Example:
Heavy Rain:A thriller with multiple perspectives and endings shaped by player decisions.
