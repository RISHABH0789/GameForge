Battle Royale Games

Overview:
Battle Royale games combine survival, exploration, and scavenging mechanics with large-scale multiplayer competition. Typically, dozens to hundreds of players are dropped into a shrinking map with limited resources. The primary goal is to be the last person or team standing. This genre exploded in popularity because of its mix of competitive tension, social teamwork, and replayability.
