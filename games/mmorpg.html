MMORPG (Massively Multiplayer Online Role-Playing Games)


Overview:
MMORPGs are vast online worlds where thousands of players coexist. Players create characters, progress through levels, complete quests, and interact socially with others. Unlike single-player RPGs, MMORPGs thrive on community—guilds, trading, raiding, and player-vs-player battles create a shared, living world. They’re long-term experiences, often requiring years of dedication, and are continually updated with expansions. These games mix exploration, story, and progression with strong social bonds.


Example:
World of Warcraft:The most iconic MMORPG, famous for its deep lore and massive community.
