Shooter Games

Overview:
Shooters focus on ranged combat, usually with firearms. They can be first-person (FPS) or third-person. Precision, reflexes, and tactical awareness are crucial. Many shooters blend story campaigns with competitive multiplayer, offering both narrative-driven and skill-based experiences. Modern shooters often include progression systems, battle passes, and massive online communities.


Example:
Call of Duty-Warzone:A free-to-play battle royale shooter with realistic gunplay and massive maps.
