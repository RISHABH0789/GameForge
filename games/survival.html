Survival Games

Overview:
Survival games drop players into hostile environments with minimal resources. The challenge is to endure—maintaining health, hunger, stamina, and often sanity. Crafting is a major feature: players must create tools, shelters, and weapons to survive. Enemies may be wildlife, other players, or supernatural threats. These games are open-ended, with no set win condition—the goal is simply to keep going as long as possible.


Example:
The Forest:A survival horror game with crafting and base-building in a cannibal-filled island.
