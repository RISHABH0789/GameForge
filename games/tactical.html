Tactical Role-Playing Games (TRPGs)

Overview:
TRPGs merge turn-based strategy with RPG character progression. Battles occur on grid-based maps where positioning and tactics matter as much as character levels. Outside combat, players grow their team, manage equipment, and engage in story-driven narratives. They are slower-paced but deeply strategic, rewarding long-term planning and team synergy.


Example:
Fire Emblem-Three Houses:Combines tactical combat with deep character relationships.
